Sarah Plane

The Abridged Version

I was born in California.

I grew up in Boise, Idaho.

I moved to Shanghai, China when I was 18.

I returned to Boise for my 1st degree: B.A. Linguistics.

I moved to Ota-shi, Gunma, Japan and taught English at NOVA.

I moved to Canberra, Australia then Sydney, Australia for my 2nd degree: M.A. Linguistics.

I spent six months in Yunnan, China recording previously undocumented, endangered languages.

I traveled to Thailand, Singapore, Fiji, New Zealand, Malaysia, and the Bahamas. Don't stop me now!

I am back in my hometown Boise, Idaho getting my 3rd degree: B.S. Computer Science, and then another Masters.

I love volunteering with different organizations, especially ones working with refugees, kids, or puppies.

I read a lot of SciFi, but sometimes I crack open books about sailing, animals, hardship, or adventure.

I can't pick a 'favorite' of most things because I enjoy variety, versatility, and variegation.

I have a crazy big American-Lebanese family, think 'My Big-Fat Greek Wedding'.

I married my highschool sweetheart, I'd be completely lost without him!

I love the beach and the ocean, it's my ultimate happy place.

I am a dog person, because cats are weird.

I really like the color green.
